About We are Sweet

Welcome to We are Sweet — Were the service is just as sweet as your favorite pastry. We are Sweet has been a warm part of the community for the past five years, proudly serving fresh, homemade treats to neighbors and friends. As a small local bakery, we’re committed to quality, kindness, and connection. Beyond baking, our team loves giving back — regularly volunteering at local events, supporting community programs, and supporting our local farmers. At We are Sweet, we’re more than a bakery — we’re a place where community and good food come together.
